## Notes for the researcher
- Prefer reading an actual SKILL.md over a list entry's blurb — the blurb oversells.
- Quality is uneven; treat mega-bundles ("1000+ skills") as leads, not endorsements.
- When you cite a related skill, capture: repo, path, what it does, and the one idea worth borrowing.
